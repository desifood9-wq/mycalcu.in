₹35 LPA In-Hand Salary — What to Expect

💡 At ₹35 LPA, monthly in-hand is approximately ₹1,90,000–₹2,10,000 under the new regime with 40% basic. Exact amount depends on salary structure, EPF, and professional tax.

₹35 LPA places you in the 30% tax bracket. Under the new regime, EPF is capped at ₹1,800/month (statutory ceiling on ₹15,000 basic). Tax planning becomes important at this level — NPS, home loan, and HRA can significantly impact take-home.

How is ₹35 LPA In-Hand Calculated?

Monthly In-Hand = (35,00,000 − Employer PF − Gratuity) ÷ 12 − Employee PF − PT − TDS

At 40% basic, new regime: Basic ₹1400000/year. EPF: ₹1,800/month (capped). Monthly gross: ₹291,666. TDS: approximately ₹98000/month.

⚠️ Tax slabs are for FY 2025-26.
