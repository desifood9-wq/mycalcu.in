₹10 Lakh Loan — EMI and Interest Explained

💡 ₹10 lakh at 8.5% for 20 years = EMI of ₹8,678/month. Total interest paid: ₹1,08,272. Use the rate slider to enter your lender's actual rate.

₹10 lakh is a common loan amount for personal loans, two-wheeler loans, small home renovation loans, and education loans. At this amount, even a 1% difference in interest rate saves ₹6,000–₹8,000 over the full tenure — worth comparing lenders.

For personal loans at ₹10 lakh, rates typically range from 10–18% p.a. For home loans, 8–9.5%. Always compare the APR (Annual Percentage Rate) which includes processing fees, not just the stated interest rate.

How is ₹10 Lakh Loan EMI Calculated?

EMI = P × r × (1+r)ⁿ / ((1+r)ⁿ − 1)

Where P = ₹10,00,000, r = monthly rate, n = tenure in months.
