7 LPA In Hand Salary — What to Expect

A ₹7 LPA package sits right at the boundary of the Section 87A rebate under the new tax regime — making it a particularly important bracket to understand. For FY 2025-26, the rebate covers tax on income up to ₹7 lakh, so a ₹7 LPA earner may pay little to zero income tax depending on their exact taxable income after the standard deduction.

After EPF and professional tax, most ₹7 LPA employees take home between ₹52,000 and ₹56,000 per month — significantly better than what the gross number of ₹58,333/month might suggest after all deductions.

How is 7 LPA In-Hand Salary Calculated?

Monthly In-Hand = Gross Monthly − EPF − Professional Tax − TDS

The 87A rebate at 7 LPA

Under the new regime, after the ₹75,000 standard deduction, taxable income on ₹7 LPA gross (after employer PF exclusion) is approximately ₹5.5–₹6 lakh — well within the ₹7 lakh rebate threshold. Result: zero or near-zero income tax.

Key deductions at ₹7 LPA

With 40% basic (₹2.8 LPA = ₹23,333/month), EPF is ₹2,800/month. Professional tax ₹200/month. TDS likely ₹0.
