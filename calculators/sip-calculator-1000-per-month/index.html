₹1,000 Per Month SIP — Building Wealth on a Tight Budget

💡 ₹1,000/month for 25 years at 12% = ₹18.9 lakh. Total invested: ₹3 lakh. Compounding multiplies your money 6.3× over 25 years.

₹1,000 per month is one of the most popular SIP amounts in India — accessible for salaried individuals earning ₹15,000–₹25,000/month and students with part-time income. It's the sweet spot between being meaningful and being affordable.

At this amount, consistency is everything. Missing just 6 months of SIP in a 20-year period can reduce your final corpus by ₹1–₹2 lakh due to lost compounding on those installments.
