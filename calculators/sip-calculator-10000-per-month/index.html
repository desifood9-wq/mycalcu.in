₹10,000 Per Month SIP — Serious Wealth Creation

💡 ₹10,000/month for 20 years at 12% = ₹99.9 lakh — nearly ₹1 crore. Invest ₹24 lakh and walk away with ₹1 crore. This is the power of disciplined SIP at a meaningful amount.

₹10,000 per month is the SIP amount where wealth creation becomes genuinely transformational. At this level, a 20-year SIP at 12% nearly reaches ₹1 crore — a milestone most Indians aspire to. It's typically suited for professionals earning ₹80,000–₹1.5 lakh/month.

At ₹10,000/month, the choice of fund matters more than at lower amounts. The difference between a 10% and 14% return over 20 years is ₹40+ lakh on this SIP amount — making fund selection and regular portfolio review genuinely important.
