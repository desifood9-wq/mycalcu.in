What Can ₹2,000/Month SIP Give You?

💡 ₹2,000/month SIP at 12% for 10 years = ₹464,678 corpus. Invested: ₹240,000. Returns: ₹224,678.

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) is the most accessible way to invest in mutual funds. ₹2,000/month is a good starting point for first-time investors. The power of compounding means starting early matters more than the amount.

At 12% annual returns (historical equity fund average), ₹2,000/month grows significantly over 15–20 years. Remember: mutual fund returns are market-linked — past performance doesn't guarantee future returns.

SIP Corpus Formula for ₹2,000/Month

Corpus = P × [((1+r)ⁿ − 1) / r] × (1+r)

Where P = ₹2,000, r = monthly return (12%÷12 = 1%), n = months.

10 years: ₹464,678 | 15 years: ₹1,009,151 | 20 years: ₹1,998,295

⚠️ Returns are market-linked and not guaranteed. Use conservative 10% for planning.
