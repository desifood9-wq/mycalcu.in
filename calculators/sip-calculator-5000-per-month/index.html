₹5,000 Per Month SIP — The Middle-Class Wealth Builder

💡 ₹5,000/month for 20 years at 12% = ₹49.9 lakh. Invest ₹12 lakh total over 20 years and walk away with nearly ₹50 lakh. That's the compounding effect at work.

₹5,000 per month is the most popular SIP amount among salaried professionals in India — typically representing 5–10% of monthly take-home for someone earning ₹50,000–₹1 lakh/month. It's the amount most financial planners recommend as a starting SIP for someone in their late 20s or early 30s.

At this SIP amount, the 20-year milestone is significant — crossing ₹50 lakh is achievable at 12% returns. For retirement planning, combining ₹5,000/month SIP with a 10% annual step-up creates a corpus exceeding ₹3 crore over 25 years.
