₹8 LPA In-Hand Salary — What to Expect in FY 2025-26

💡 At ₹8 LPA, monthly in-hand is approximately ₹61,432–₹67,899 under the new regime with 40% basic. Zero income tax applies — taxable income is within the ₹12L 87A rebate limit.

For ₹8 LPA CTC with 40% basic salary structure, here is what to expect:

  • Gross monthly salary: ₹66,666
  • Employee EPF (12% of basic): ₹1,800/month
  • Professional tax: ₹200/month (varies by state)
  • Income tax (new regime): ₹0 (87A rebate — zero tax)
  • Monthly in-hand: ~₹64,666

Actual in-hand salary depends on your exact salary structure, city, and whether your company contributes EPF on full basic or ₹15,000 ceiling. Use the calculator above for your precise figure.

How is ₹8 LPA In-Hand Salary Calculated?

Monthly In-Hand = (CTC − Employer PF − Gratuity) ÷ 12 − Employee PF − PT − TDS

Step-by-step for ₹8 LPA

Step 1: Gross monthly = ₹66,666
Step 2: Basic (40%) = ₹26,666/month
Step 3: Employee EPF = ₹1,800/month
Step 4: Professional tax = ₹200/month
Step 5: TDS = ₹0 (zero tax under 87A rebate)
Monthly in-hand = ~₹64,666

⚠️ Tax slabs are for FY 2025-26. Verify after each Union Budget.
